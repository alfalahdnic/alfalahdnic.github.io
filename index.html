About AL-FALAH

Al Falah Friends is the student union of Darunnajath Islamic Complex, promoting unity, leadership, and student participation in academic, cultural, and social activities.

Al Falah Friends, the official class union of Darunnajath Islamic Complex, Vallapuzha, is a dynamic group formed to inspire leadership, teamwork, and positive change among students. Through various educational, cultural, and community-centered programs, the union promotes a spirit of brotherhood, service, and responsibility. It provides students with opportunities to voice their ideas, organize events, and contribute meaningfully to campus life, making it a vital part of the institution’s student development vision.
